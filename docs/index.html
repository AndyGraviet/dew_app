Features

📝

Task Management

Create, organize, and track your tasks with an intuitive interface. Set priorities, due dates, and never miss important deadlines.

🎯

Focus Timer

Built-in Pomodoro timer to help you stay focused and productive. Customize work and break intervals to match your rhythm.

📊

Progress Tracking

Visual insights into your productivity patterns. Track completed tasks, focus time, and celebrate your achievements.

🤝

Team Collaboration

Share lists with friends and colleagues. Give kudos for completed tasks and stay motivated together.

🔄

Auto-Updates

Always stay up-to-date with the latest features and improvements. Updates are delivered seamlessly in the background.

🌟

Beautiful Design

Crafted with attention to detail. Glass morphism design that adapts to your system's appearance preferences.
